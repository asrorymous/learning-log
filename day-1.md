## 💡 Reflection & Pivot

_Date: 03 March 2026_

After deep-diving into scraping, I realized that my core interest lies in the **architecture and reliability of data flow**, rather than the constant maintenance of fragile web selectors.

**The Pivot:**
I am officially shifting my focus from **Web Scraping** to **ELT (Extract, Load, Transform) Pipelines**.

### Why the change?

1. **Stability**: Scraping is highly dependent on UI changes; ELT focuses on structured data contracts (APIs/DBs).
2. **Scalability**: I want to build systems that handle massive datasets using Modern Data Stack tools.
3. **Architecture**: I am more fascinated by how data is modeled and transformed (dbt/SQL) than how it is "captured" from a website.

_This "Day 1" remains here as a testament to my starting point, but my journey continues towards Data Engineering._
